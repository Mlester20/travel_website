It's more fun in the Philippines

Baguio City

Baguio City, located in the northern part of the Philippines, is a popular tourist destination known for its cool climate, scenic mountain views, and rich cultural heritage. Also known as the "City of Pines," Baguio offers a unique blend of natural beauty, history, and modern amenities that make it a must-visit destination for travelers.

Vigan Ilocos

Vigan, located in the province of Ilocos Sur in the Philippines, is a charming city that's rich in history and culture. Known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture, Vigan was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1999.

Palawan

Palawan is a tropical paradise located in the southwestern part of the Philippines. It is often considered as one of the most beautiful islands in the world, and it's not hard to see why. With its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, limestone cliffs, and lush forests, Palawan offers a wide range of natural attractions for visitors to enjoy

Luneta Park Manila

Luneta Park, also known as Rizal Park, is a historic urban park located in the heart of Manila, the capital city of the Philippines. The park is named after the country's national hero, Jose Rizal, who was executed on the site where the park now stands

7 Stones Boracay

Cebu is a beautiful island province located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It is home to many breathtaking travel destinations, including seven must-see natural attractions known as the Seven Stone Travel Spot.

Batanes

Batanes may be the Philippines’ smallest and least populous province, but don’t let that fool you into thinking that there’s nothing to see in this picturesque destination. It is one of the top tourist destinations in the Philippines and is home to some of the best islands in the Philippines
